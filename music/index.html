Music Projects

A collection of music projects by me and those that I have been part of. I play guitar and keyboards primarily, sometimes trumpet (but not nearly as much as I used to) and sometimes some vocals too. The last few years I've been primarily writing and recording; expanding my engineering, production, recording, and mixing skills. Examples of which you can check out below.

Elias Hull - Horizons EP (2022)

"Horizons" is my second EP released under the pseudonym Elias Hull. Released April 1, 2022, "Horizons" is a gazey, dreampop EP written, recorded, engineered, produced, mixed, and mastered all by me! A limited edition tape cassette is also available for a shot time on Bandcamp.

Available on: Bandcamp, Spotify, Soundcloud, Apple Music and many, many more.

Photo: @keslerbear

Elias Hull - Matter of Perspective EP (2018)

"Matter of Perspective" is my debut EP under the pseudonym Elias Hull. Released November 20, 2018, "Matter of Perspective" is an indie rock EP written, recorded, engineered, and produced all by me!

Available on: Bandcamp, Spotify, Soundcloud, Apple Music

Photo: Lex Anderson

Broken Bodies Club EP (2018)

Broken Bodies Club was the eponymous debut EP from a recording project consisting of me, Marissa Miller, and Alex Gilvarry. Recorded between 2016 and 2017 we released three complete songs we made in 2018 with album artwork by Miranda Gilvarry.

Available on: Bandcamp, Spotify, Soundcloud, Apple Music

Photo: @jennifercarter3

L'anarchiste - Giant (2015)

L'anarchiste's debut full-length album "Giant" was released on Kilby Records on July 14th, 2015. I played guitar, keyboards, banjo, trumpet, and vocals live with L'anarchiste beginning in 2014. The album received coverage by The Wall Street Journal, NPR, The Daily Herald, SLUG Magazine, Salt Lake City Weekly, PopMatters, among others.

Available on: Bandcamp, Spotify, Soundcloud, Apple Music

Photo: @jennifercarter3

Skills

My experience encompasses numerous aspects of musicianship. From performance, to recording, to production, engineering, mixing, mastering, and composition. I also have experience producing podcasts, and other mediums of audio. My primary tools are Apple's Logic Pro X and Adobe Audition.
